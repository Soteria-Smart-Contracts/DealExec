Embrace Flexibility and Unlock Your Potential as an Independent Executive Deal Advisor 

July 2023


Are you a seasoned executive or transactional advisor seeking to leverage your expertise in strategic deal
making? Imagine a pathway that grants you the freedom to manage your own schedule, work remotely
on your terms, and collaborate with a diverse client base across various industries. At DEALEXEC, we
value the independence and entrepreneurial spirit of our executive deal advisors, empowering them to
reach their full potential while enjoying the benefits of flexibility and a rewarding compensation
structure. Embark on a journey that redefines your professional success as an independent executive
deal advisor.

Unleash Your Potential with Flexible Scheduling

Bid farewell to rigid office hours and embrace a dynamic role that seamlessly integrates with your
lifestyle. As an independent executive deal advisor with DEALEXEC, you have the autonomy to design a
schedule that best suits your preferences. We understand the value of your expertise, and we trust you
to manage your time efficiently. Whether you prefer early morning strategy sessions, late-night
negotiations, or a mix of both, our flexible approach allows you to curate your client engagements and
discover a fulfilling work-life balance.

Redefining Advisory Services: Embracing Remote Work

The future of advisory services is rapidly evolving with the growing trend of remote work, granting
experienced professionals the freedom to work from any location. At DEALEXEC, we embrace this
transformative shift and wholeheartedly support independent advisors who seek to leverage technology
for seamless remote collaboration. While most engagements can be conducted remotely, we understand
that certain deals may require in-person meetings or on-site presence at clients' offices or deal locations.
As an independent contractor with DEALEXEC, our executive deal advisors have the adaptability to cater
to specific in-person requirements when necessary.

Engage with a Diverse Client Base

At DEALEXEC, we take pride in developing a diverse client base spanning multiple industries. Our
executive deal advisors handle a rich tapestry of business challenges, deal structures, and cultural
nuances. They have worked with clients across various sectors, including technology, finance, healthcare,
energy, and more. This diversity not only enriches their professional experience but also broadens their
network and fosters continuous learning.
