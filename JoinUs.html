Join DEALEXEC as an Executive Deal Advisor

Are you an experienced senior executive or transactional advisor looking for a rewarding opportunity to
guide and lead complex commercial transactions and business negotiations for our clients? Look no
further than DEALEXEC! We are an entrepreneurial firm that provides our clients with on-demand access
to the most experienced executive-level deal advisors. Our advisors’ mission is to deliver executive-level
leadership and support to client organizations seeking to navigate their most intricate business
transactions.

Our advisors work directly with client organizations, in many cases on a remote basis with opportunities
to travel to a clients’ site or other locations where necessary to provide the guidance and direction
needed to bring a deal to completion. This provides them with flexibility to arrange their schedules and
work arrangements in a manner that balances our clients’ needs and their personal preferences, and in
many cases allows them to work remotely from anywhere in the world. Our compensation
arrangements afford our advisors the opportunity to set their own compensation levels for a particular
mandate, whether on a weekly, monthly or other basis, based on the advisor’s preferences and each
client’s requirements.

Why Choose DEALEXEC?

At DEALEXEC, we understand that strategic deal making requires exceptional leadership experience,
expertise and guidance, as well as the personal skills to manage our clients’ internal and external
resources. That's why our team of executive deal advisors specializes in providing comprehensive
transactional support tailored to each client’s unique needs. Whether you are experienced working with
start-ups, mid-size or large multinational corporations, if you have the experience, knowledge and skills
to assist and guide our clients throughout every step of their commercial transactions and negotiations,
we want to hear from you.
Our Expertise
Our executive deal advisors possess extensive experience in strategic deal making and have successfully
led organizations through various complex business transactions. From mergers and acquisitions to
joint ventures, debt and equity financings, and other critical business transactions, our advisors have a
deep understanding of critical business situations. They offer strategic leadership and guidance from
the initial planning and structuring stage of a transaction to the execution and implementation of our
clients’ deals. Some of our advisors have backgrounds in legal, financial and/or management consulting
roles with a focus on transactional advice, strategy and deal execution, while others have extensive
senior executive experience leading organizations through complex transactions.

Client-Centric Approach

At DEALEXEC, we prioritize our clients' success and satisfaction while enabling our advisors to structure
their work arrangements in a flexible manner that addresses both the client’s needs and the advisor’s
personal preferences. Our executive deal advisors work seamlessly and in many cases remotely with our
client’s senior management and internal resources, leading multidisciplinary teams and coordinating
with external tax, legal, accounting, and other professional advisors. They ensure effective deal strategy,
structuring, due diligence, negotiation, and closing. Our advisors aim to achieve optimal results through
creative thinking, streamlined processes, proactive communication, and unwavering integrity.

International Reach

We understand the importance of global and cross-border transactions and have vast experience
working with clients and transactions in multiple jurisdictions. Our executive deal advisors have
experience working in international business environments and across cultural boundaries, including in
transactions in the United States, Canada, Mexico, Europe, the United Kingdom, the United Arab
Emirates, Africa, and Australia.
